D.I.N.A 1.0

I will get D.I.N.A to work with an mqtt broker known as HiveMQ, this will allow me to make commands from many devices making it easier to expand in the future with D.I.N.A 2.0.

This version requires basic functionality (light controls, curtain controls, time, etc) to work and an MQTT connection to be present.

D.I.N.A 2.0

I will set up an old computer that will use a linux based Operating System with the code to D.I.N.A 1.0, this will give me more processing power for bigger commands that involve linking existing commands together e.g (turn on and off lights and open curtains).

This will also be a good opertunity to set up my own private MQTT broker and investigate the many ways I can communicate data securily through the connected devices.

D.I.N.A 3.0

I will connect D.I.N.A to my Google Calender which will allow me to set tasks and reminders via my voice, I will also research the equipment necessary to design a functional smart mirror that is capable of displaying my schedule.

This functionality may require D.I.N.A to be capable of discovering other devices on the network and request connection. It is possible that face recognition may be required to detect who is standing in front of the smart mirror to display their schedule.
